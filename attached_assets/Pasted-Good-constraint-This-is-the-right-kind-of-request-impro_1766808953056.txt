2️⃣ Progressive Reveal Animation (Key Improvement)

Instead of everything loading at once, guide the eye.

Animation Sequence (500–700ms total)
	1.	Background fades in (already good)
	2.	“Your Zone Flow” text fades + slides up (opacity 0 → 1, y +8px → 0)
	3.	Cards appear one by one (80ms stagger)
	4.	Primary CTA pulses once (scale 1 → 1.04 → 1)

This creates direction without flash.

6️⃣ Micro-Interactions That Add Life (But Stay Calm)

Hover States
	•	Cards: soft glow + lift

	•	Icons: fade in, not slide

Click Feedback

	•	Page transition → crossfade, not slide

Nothing playful. Everything deliberate.
