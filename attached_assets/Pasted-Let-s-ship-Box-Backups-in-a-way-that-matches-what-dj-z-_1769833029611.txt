Landing page section you can paste into dj-z-s.box

Box Backups

Encrypted backups you control. Restore anytime.

DJZS is local-first: your device stays the source of truth. Box Backups are an optional way to export an encrypted snapshot of your vault to decentralized storage.

What you get
	•	Local-first by default — your vault lives on your device  ￼
	•	Encrypted before upload — you hold the key (always)
	•	Verifiable receipts — export includes a hash/root so you can prove what was backed up (without exposing your journal)

Buttons (CTAs)
	•	Create a Box Backup
	•	How Box Works

Footnote (important)
Box Backups currently use Base mainnet via Jackal EVM Outposts (beta).  ￼

The “fits the brand” constraints you should keep

These are the rules that keep Box consistent with dj-z-s.box’s current promise:
	1.	Always say “optional backup,” never “sync.”
Your homepage is clearly “local-first / you write first.” Keep that intact.  ￼
	2.	Be explicit about Base-only + beta.
Jackal’s EVM Outpost docs say Base mainnet only and “open beta.”  ￼
	3.	Show a provider list link in Advanced.
The docs point to an active providers endpoint; you can even surface it as “View active providers.”  ￼
