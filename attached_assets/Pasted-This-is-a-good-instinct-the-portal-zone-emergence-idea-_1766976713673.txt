1️⃣ Visual Concept: “Coming Out of a Zone”

Think of the circles not as decoration, but as layers of a system:
	•	The text = the thought
	•	The circles = the zone / boundary / context
	•	The blur + glow = transition between zones

You want it to feel like:

This clarity is emerging from a structured space.

⸻

2️⃣ Design Rules (Keep It Elegant, Not Sci-Fi)

✅ Use 3–5 circles max

More than that becomes noise.

✅ Circles should be:
	•	Soft-edged (blurred)
	•	Low contrast
	•	Behind the text (never competing)

❌ Avoid:
	•	Hard outlines
	•	Bright neon
	•	Fast motion
	•	Perfect symmetry

DJZS = calm, not cyberpunk.

⸻

3️⃣ Circle Styles That Work Best for DJZS

Primary Portal Circle (Core Zone)
	•	Large
	•	Slight purple / indigo
	•	Heavy blur
	•	Low opacity

bg-purple-500/10
blur-3xl

Secondary Rings (Structure)
	•	Slightly offset
	•	Even lower opacity
	•	Larger radius

bg-indigo-400/5
blur-2xl

Optional Halo (Clarity)
	•	White or lavender
	•	Very subtle
	•	Only behind keywords like CLARITY or DECENTRALIZED

⸻

4️⃣ Layout Pattern (Very Important)

The circles should be:
	•	Centered slightly ABOVE the text
	•	So the text feels like it’s rising out of the zone

Think:

     ◯
   ◯     ◯
        TEXT

Not:

TEXT
◯ ◯ ◯
